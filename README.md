## Investment Thesis
1. **AI Growth Engine:** NVIDIA captures > 80 % of the AI‑GPU market; Blackwell expected to cement leadership through higher performance and energy efficiency.
2. **Financial Strength:** Massive cash pile, low leverage, robust free‑cash‑flow generation enables continued buybacks and R&D spend.
3. **Valuation:** Forward PE ~70× remains premium but justified by multi‑year growth trajectory; upside ~10 % per consensus.
4. **Risks:** Regulatory export restrictions, high valuation, competitive pressure from AMD/Intel and emerging open‑source AI models.

**Recommendation:** Maintain a **Buy** stance with a target of **$155** and monitor regulatory developments and any slowdown in AI‑related spend.
